Academia Society

Spread Words, Spread Education

Vision

Academia Society aims to bring about a paradigm shift in the education. We see education as a lifelong process. And, education should not only bring out the best in every individual in a very natural way but it should also prepare the individuals as the active facilitator and promoter of knowedge and truth. Education should teach individuals to hear a new perspective without any prejudice. It should develop a temper of reasoning in individual to discern the right and wrong. It should nurture the spirit of enquiry and curiosity in every individual. And, most importantly, education should equip individuals with capability to earn for their livelihood but also prepare them capable enough to be the change and bring about a new change.

Academia society aims to create an ecosystem which shall enable every individual to participate actively and contribute meaningfully to the overall education system.

In this direction, Academia society is focussing on having open libraries in every village of the country, which shall not only expose the individuals to the vast sea of knowledge but which shall also work as a channel to percolate informations relating to the education to every section of the society. Also, these libraries shall provide a platform for active and engaging discourses which will in turn bring out the spirit of enquiry in individuals.
